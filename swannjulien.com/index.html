About me

Hi!

After spending many hours watching tutorials and learning the basics of front-end development I am delighted to show you here my very first website.

This story begins 10 years ago when I followed a French MOOC about how to use Photoshop for photo edition and graphic design. I was amazed to see how much we can learn for free and I really enjoyed mastering new skills.

Then in 2019, I was in a moment of my life where I had some free time. One of these really rare moments! So I decided to take this opportunity to make this website for fun and to learn HTML and CSS.

At the end, what was once a hobby has become my new profession. So I hope you’ll enjoy surfing this webpage as much as I enjoyed creating it.
